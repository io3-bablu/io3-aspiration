    Precautions

Q. What kind of precautions should I follow
during the trip?

A. There are 5 rules to avoid Coronavirus. You should follow and apply this to your loved ones
during the trip.

  1. Always wear a mask in a crowded place.
  2. Wash your hands from both sides with good quality handwash certified by medical professionals.
  3. Carry a sanitizer with you after touching anything then sanitize your hands properly.
  4. Maintain at least one-meter distance with everybody if not possible then leave that place immediately and search for the safe place.
  5. If you and your tripmates like friends, or a family member feel something awkward then immediately call for medical help and get checked.
